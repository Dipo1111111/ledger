? Rules

Objective

Be the last corporation remaining. Players are eliminated only through Hostile Takeover failure or Financial Collapse. No dice, no luck — every victory is earned.

Turn Structure

Each turn: Collect Income (automatic from all assets) → Actions (buy, sell, borrow, invest, negotiate, auction, takeover) → End Turn.

Each asset pays itself back in ~4 profitable rounds. Sell to Bank at 50% of purchase price (emergency only).

Taxes

After every full round (all players have played), everyone pays taxes simultaneously based on owned assets. If you cannot pay, you enter Financial Distress — sell, borrow, or seek investments to recover.

Loans

Borrow up to 120 LC anytime during your turn. 50% interest, repay within 3 rounds. One active loan at a time. Default → Financial Distress → potential Bankruptcy.

Investments

Any player can invest in another. Terms are fully negotiated — amount, repayment, deadline, conditions. All deals are enforced by the game. No scams.

Hostile Takeovers

Requires 120 LC minimum wealth. Pay 40 LC filing fee (nonrefundable). Target gets one full round to defend. If they reach the required LC reserve, the takeover fails. If they can't, they're eliminated — attacker takes one asset + small reward.

Corporate Expansion

Once most assets are player-owned, anyone can call a vote. Simple majority passes. YES voters each pay 10 LC. A fresh batch of assets enters the market. Maximum one expansion per game.

Elimination & Bankruptcy

If you cannot meet a mandatory payment (taxes, loan repayment, takeover defense) after exhausting all recovery options, you're eliminated. No score — only survival.
