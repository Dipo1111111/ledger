# Ledger — Project Overview

## What is Ledger?

Ledger is a **competitive online multiplayer business strategy game** where players build corporate empires through investing, asset acquisition, financial management, negotiation, calculated risk-taking, and corporate warfare.

Unlike Monopoly — which is driven by movement and luck — Ledger is designed entirely around **player decisions**. There are no dice, no random movement, no chance cards. Every victory feels earned.

## The Problem It Solves

Most digital board games fall into one of two traps:
1. **Too much luck** (Monopoly, Risk) — the winner is often decided by dice rolls, not skill
2. **Too dry** (purely economic simulators) — no social dynamics, no memorable moments

Ledger sits in the gap: a **strategy game disguised as a business game** where player interaction, negotiation, and calculated risk create stories people remember and want to replay.

## Core Experience

Players should feel like **founders**, not accountants. The emotional journey:
- **Excitement** → building something
- **Growth** → expanding their corporation
- **Risk** → taking loans, making investments
- **Pressure** → taxes, competition
- **Crisis** → hostile takeovers, potential bankruptcy
- **Recovery** → surviving against odds
- **Victory** → outplaying everyone

Or: **Collapse** → learning from failure.

## Who Is It For?

| Audience | Why They'll Play |
|----------|-----------------|
| Strategy gamers | Deep economic decisions, no luck |
| Friend groups | Social negotiation, memorable stories |
| Chess/poker players | Skill-based competition, psychology |
| Finance enthusiasts | Economic systems, portfolio building |
| Streamers / content creators | Player-driven drama, lobbies |

## Target Audience

- **Age:** 13+
- **Players:** 2–6 (recommended 4–6)
- **Match length:** 30–60 minutes
- **Platform:** Web (desktop-first, mobile responsive)

## Win Condition

Become the **last corporation remaining**. Players are eliminated only through Hostile Takeover failure or Financial Collapse. No scores. Only survival.

## Currency

**Ledger Chips (LC)** — the single universal currency. Everything uses LC: assets, loans, investments, taxes, takeovers, trading.

## Design Philosophy

> "If a mechanic removes meaningful decisions — remove it. If a mechanic creates meaningful decisions — keep it."

This single rule guides every feature. See `architecture.md` for system boundaries and `ui-rules.md` for visual design.
